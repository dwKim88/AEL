Welcome to Applied Electromagnetics Laboratory!

Our research focuses on wireless power transfer system, magnetic field reduction, embedded system based system control. The wireless power tranfser based powering is applied on microrobot, implantable medical devices, electric vehicles. We have several openings for graduate students and undergraduate students. If you are interested in working with us, please contact to Prof. Dongwook Kim (dwkim@yu.ac.kr) with your research interests and Curriculum Vitae.
